The Miracle of Tungsten

Tungsten, number 74 on the periodic table. It is a very interesting metal due to its unique qualities. Its atomic symbol is W whitch originates from its other name Wolfram. It is a rare element found on earth almost only as already mixed, it is also a notoriously hard metal to work with. It has an extremely high melting point, making it the most heat resistant of all metals. Additionally it is very dense, coming in seventh place for most dense element, and its chemical makup makes it extremely acid resistant. Making both strong acid and strong bases harmless at normal tempratures, however acids or bases can have an effect if used on red-hot tungston as the heat allows for a much stronger reaction.
